> ### What changed
> 1. **Operator identified.** Added "Blue Pelican Digital LLC" as the entity operating the App.
> 2. **Contact email corrected.** Replaced the WordPress display placeholder `[email protected]` with the real public address `support@urbanquestapp.com`.
> 3. **NEW Section 8 — Prohibited Content & Conduct.** Apple App Store Review Guideline 1.2 and Google Play's UGC policy **require** apps that host user-generated content to publish a clear list of prohibited content categories and reserve the right to remove and ban. The live version had a basic "User Conduct" section but did not enumerate prohibited content. **This is the most important addition for compliance.**

> 6. **Sections renumbered** to accommodate the new sections (the existing numbering 8–16 becomes 10–19).
> 7. **Section 2 (App Description) note removed.** Removed the "personal, non-commercial use only" language because the App allows creators to publish quests, which is creative/commercial use of the platform. Replaced with a more accurate description of permitted use.
