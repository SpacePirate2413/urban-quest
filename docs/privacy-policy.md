# Privacy Policy – Urban Quest

**Effective Date:** April 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how **Blue Pelican Digital LLC** ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application Urban Quest (the "App"), a location-based interactive storytelling experience that uses GPS to guide users through real-world locations. This policy is intended to comply with **Apple App Store privacy and location disclosure requirements** and **Google Play data-safety requirements**.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### a. Personal Information

We collect personal information that you provide when you sign in or interact with the App, including:

- **Account identifiers** (Apple ID or Google ID) used to authenticate your sign-in
- **Email address** associated with your Apple or Google account, used for support and account-related communication
- **Display name or username** if you choose to set one

The App requires sign-in to access core functionality (saving quest progress, syncing across devices, and creator features). Sign-in is performed exclusively through Sign in with Apple or Sign in with Google; we do not collect or store passwords directly.

### b. Location Information (Required for Core Functionality)

The App collects **precise location information (GPS)** to enable its core functionality.

We use your location data to:

- Guide you through story locations and real-world stops
- Unlock and trigger story content when you arrive at specific locations
- Ensure accurate progression through the experience

**Location data is collected only while the App is in use (foreground)** and is not used for advertising or marketing purposes.

If you do not allow location access, the App will not function as intended.

### c. User-Generated Content

If you create or publish content within the App (for example, quests, scenes, narration, or media uploads via our creator tools), we collect and store that content as necessary to make it available to other users. By submitting user-generated content, you grant us the licenses described in our Terms & Conditions.

### d. Usage and Diagnostic Data

We may automatically collect limited non-identifying information, including:

- App interactions and story progress
- Device type and operating system
- Crash logs and performance diagnostics

This information is used solely to improve App stability and user experience.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- Operate and provide the App's core features
- Deliver location-based story content
- Authenticate sign-in and maintain your account
- Provide customer support
- Improve performance, reliability, and usability
- Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

We do **not** use personal or location data for targeted advertising.

## 3. Payments and Subscriptions

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed through the **Apple App Store** (on iOS) or **Google Play** (on Android) and managed using **RevenueCat**.

We do not collect or store your full payment information. Payment data is handled directly by Apple, Google, and RevenueCat in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited information only in the following cases:

- **Service Providers:** With trusted third-party providers (such as RevenueCat) strictly for App functionality and purchase management
- **Legal Compliance:** If required by law or valid legal process
- **Business Transfers:** In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

All third parties are required to safeguard your data and use it only for authorized purposes.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain personal and location-related data only for as long as necessary to provide the App and comply with legal obligations. Location data is not stored long-term unless required for functionality or legal compliance.

If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your associated personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., fraud prevention or financial records).

## 6. Your Privacy Choices

You may:

- **Enable or disable location access** at any time through your device settings.
- **Request access to or deletion of your personal information.** To delete your account and associated personal data, email us at **support@urbanquestapp.com** with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" from the email address associated with your account, or use the in-app account deletion option in your profile settings.
- **Opt out of non-essential communications** at any time through the unsubscribe link in any email we send you.

Disabling location services will prevent the App from delivering its core experience.

## 7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or under 16 where required by law). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child without verified parental consent, we will delete it as soon as practicable.

## 8. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. However, no system is completely secure. You are responsible for keeping your Apple ID or Google account credentials secure.

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on our website or within the App with an updated effective date. Material changes will be announced through the App or via email where appropriate.

## 10. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

**Email:** support@urbanquestapp.com
**Mailing address:** Blue Pelican Digital LLC

## Apple App Store Disclosure Summary

- Location data is collected **only while the app is in use**
- Location data is required for core functionality
- Location data is not used for advertising or tracking
- Payments are processed by Apple and RevenueCat

## Google Play Store Disclosure Summary

To comply with Google Play policies, we provide the following disclosures:

- The App collects **precise location data** to enable core, user-facing functionality (location-based storytelling and navigation between story stops).
- Location access is requested only when necessary for App functionality and is not used for advertising, analytics unrelated to functionality, or data brokering.
- Location data is collected **only while the App is in use** and is not collected in the background.
- Users are clearly informed at runtime why location access is required and how it is used.
- Location data is not sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

Payments on Android devices are processed through Google Play and managed using **RevenueCat**. We do not collect or store full payment details.
