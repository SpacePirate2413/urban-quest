# Account Deletion — Urban Quest

**Last updated:** April 26, 2026

This page explains how to delete your Urban Quest account and what happens to your data when you do.

Urban Quest is operated by **Blue Pelican Digital LLC**.

---

## How to delete your account

You have two ways to delete your Urban Quest account.

### Option 1 — From inside the app (fastest)

1. Open the Urban Quest app.
2. Go to your **Profile** tab.
3. Tap **Settings → Delete Account**.
4. Confirm the deletion. Your account will be deleted immediately and you will be signed out.

### Option 2 — By email (if you have already uninstalled the app)

If you no longer have the app installed, you can request deletion by email:

1. From the email address associated with your Urban Quest account, send a message to **support@urbanquestapp.com**.
2. Use the subject line: **Account Deletion Request**.
3. Include your account display name (if you set one) so we can confirm the right account.

We respond to email-based deletion requests within **5 business days** to confirm your request and complete the deletion.

---

## What gets deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is **permanently removed** from our active systems:

- Your account record (Apple ID or Google ID identifier, email address, display name)
- Your in-app preferences and settings
- Your quest progress, completion history, and saved quests
- Any quests you authored as a Creator (your authored content will be removed from public view; see below for the limited retention exception)
- Any media you uploaded (images, audio, video)
- Direct messages and comments you posted, where applicable
- Reviews and ratings you submitted

---

## What may be retained, and why

In limited cases, we may retain some data after account deletion. We do this only where it is necessary for legal, security, or financial reasons. Specifically:

- **Financial records** (purchase history, refund records, tax-related transaction data) are retained for as long as required by applicable tax and accounting laws — typically up to **7 years** in the United States.
- **Fraud-prevention and abuse records.** If your account was involved in a confirmed policy violation, fraud incident, or safety report, we may retain the minimum data necessary to prevent the same actor from re-registering or to comply with law-enforcement requests.
- **Anonymized analytics.** Aggregated, non-identifying usage statistics (e.g., how many users completed a given quest) are not tied to your identity and are not deleted.
- **Backups.** Standard system backups may contain copies of your data for up to **30 days** after deletion. These backups are not actively used and are overwritten on a rolling basis.
- **Published Creator content.** If you authored quests that were already played and reviewed by other users, the **content itself may remain available in an anonymized form** (with your authorship, profile, and any personally identifying details removed) so that other users' play history and reviews stay intact. You can request full removal in your deletion request email and we will honor it where technically feasible.

We do not use any retained data for marketing, advertising, or profiling.

---

## How long deletion takes

- **In-app deletion** is processed immediately. Your data is removed from active systems within **24 hours**.
- **Email-based deletion** is processed within **5 business days** of our confirmation email.
- **System backups** containing your data are purged on a rolling basis and are fully removed within **30 days** of your deletion request.

---

## Third-party services

Urban Quest uses the following third-party services that may have a copy of limited data tied to your account. When you delete your account, we instruct each provider to delete the corresponding data, in accordance with their terms:

- **Apple** and **Google** (sign-in providers): we delete the link between your Apple/Google identifier and your Urban Quest account. To delete the underlying Apple ID or Google account itself, contact Apple or Google directly.
- **RevenueCat** (purchase management): purchase records are retained as required for tax and refund handling, in accordance with RevenueCat's privacy policy and our Section 5 retention policy.

---

## Children's accounts

Urban Quest is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or 16 where required by law). If you are a parent or legal guardian and believe a child has created an account, email **support@urbanquestapp.com** with the subject line **"Child Account Deletion"** and we will delete the account immediately.
