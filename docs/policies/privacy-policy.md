This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application (the "App"), a location-based interactive storytelling experience that uses GPS to guide users through real-world locations. This policy is intended to comply with **Apple App Store privacy and location disclosure requirements**.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### a. Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide, such as:

- Name or username (if applicable)
- Email address (for support or account-related communication)

We do not require account creation unless explicitly stated within the App.

### b. Location Information (Required for Core Functionality)

The App collects **precise location information (GPS)** to enable its core functionality.

We use your location data to:

- Guide you through story locations and real-world stops
- Unlock and trigger story content when you arrive at specific locations
- Ensure accurate progression through the experience

**Location data is collected only while the App is in use (foreground)** and is not used for advertising or marketing purposes.

If you do not allow location access, the App will not function as intended.

### c. Usage and Diagnostic Data

We may automatically collect limited non-identifying information, including:

- App interactions and story progress
- Device type and operating system
- Crash logs and performance diagnostics

This information is used solely to improve App stability and user experience.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- Operate and provide the App's core features
- Deliver location-based story content
- Provide customer support
- Improve performance, reliability, and usability
- Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

We do **not** use personal or location data for targeted advertising.

## 3. Payments and Subscriptions

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed through the Apple App Store and managed using **RevenueCat**.

We do not collect or store your full payment information. Payment data is handled directly by Apple and RevenueCat in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited information only in the following cases:

- **Service Providers:** With trusted third-party providers (such as RevenueCat) strictly for App functionality and purchase management
- **Legal Compliance:** If required by law or valid legal process
- **Business Transfers:** In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

All third parties are required to safeguard your data and use it only for authorized purposes.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain personal and location-related data only for as long as necessary to provide the App and comply with legal obligations. Location data is not stored long-term unless required for functionality or legal compliance.

## 6. Your Privacy Choices

You may:

- Enable or disable location access at any time through your device settings
- Request access to or deletion of your personal information, subject to applicable law

Disabling location services will prevent the App from delivering its core experience.

## 7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or under 16 where required by law). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 8. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. However, no system is completely secure.

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on our website or within the App with an updated effective date.

## Google Play Store Disclosure Summary

To comply with Google Play policies, we provide the following disclosures:

- The App collects **precise location data** to enable core, user-facing functionality (location-based storytelling and navigation between story stops).
- Location access is requested only when necessary for App functionality and is not used for advertising, analytics unrelated to functionality, or data brokering.
- Location data is collected **only while the App is in use** and is not collected in the background.
- Users are clearly informed at runtime why location access is required and how it is used.
- Location data is not sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

Payments on Android devices are processed through Google Play and managed using **RevenueCat**. We do not collect or store full payment details.
